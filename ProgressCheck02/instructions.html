Welcome to DriveWise! This game will serve to help you improve your memory
while also helping you practice for your written driver's test. Once you exit this instructions
page and click on the button that says "Game Time!" you will be lead to a page with a four-by-four
array of blue squares. There you will click on a box and click on another box. If the second box
does not match with the first then you will be prompted to try again and using your memory
you should be able to find two that match. Once you match them, you will be prompted with two
multiple choice questions pertaining to driver's ed. Depending on the amount of questions
you get right, you will be given points that will be added to your total score. Have fun!
